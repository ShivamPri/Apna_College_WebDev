The term "Avengers" most commonly refers to the Marvel superhero team and its related films, comics, and media franchise. The team is an assembly of superheroes like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and Hulk who band together to fight threats like Loki and Thanos. The group has appeared in many movies, including the 2012 film The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron
